QueersPlayGames

A collection of gaming resources for the queer community.

QueersPlayGames aims to be a collection of gaming resources relevant to individuals and communities of gender and sexual diversity. Contributions (including submissions, ideas, and requests for edits) may be made by the project issue tracker (prefered) and pull requests. Additionally you can make requests via twitter.
